About Us

Our Mission Is to Strengthen Youth Through Sports

ECHOLS DUGOUT CLUB INC helps students and families thrive by supporting athletic programs, team culture, and positive community involvement in Echols County.

Mission

Character, Commitment, and Community

We are a nonprofit dedicated to student-athlete growth. Our mission is to remove barriers to participation, encourage teamwork, and create spaces where every player has the chance to compete, learn, and lead.

Through partnerships with families, volunteers, and local supporters, we invest in the resources that keep students active and connected.
